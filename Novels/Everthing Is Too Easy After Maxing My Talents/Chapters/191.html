Chapter 191: Ancient Warrior, Resistance Camp!_3

On the left, a young man in his mid-twenties couldn't help but complain, "I can't stand it! Captain, I think we shouldn't have brought MO Fei here, right? This kid relies on his extraordinary powers and is nothing but trouble!" The three of them continued to chat while playing cards.

The middle-aged woman coughed softly, drawing the attention of the three men to the commotion.

The man in his thirties, who had been playing cards, discarded his hand and stood up. He asked, "Are the two of you the 'guests?"

Li Yueming nodded and took out the medal given to him by Han Longfei. He introduced himself, saying, "My name is Li Yueming. May I know your names?"

Upon seeing the medal, the man sighed with relief, stood up, and introduced himself as the leader of N City's Resistance Army's Third Team, Han Longyan. He pointed to the two others, mentioning the vice-leader, Qian Jun, and the elite member, MO Fei.

Han Longyan bore a resemblance to Han Longtian, and it was evident they were relatives. This connection helped maintain control over the Resistance from afar.

After some pleasantries, Han Longyan welcomed Li Yueming and Sun Ruo to the N City's Resistance Army and extended an invitation to join them at their headquarters at East Peak Mountain. Li Yueming and Sun Ruo accepted the invitation.

However, one of the team members, MO Fei, was more interested in Sun Ruo. He tried to impress her with flattery, but she dismissed his advances, making it clear she wasn't interested. MO Fei then proudly declared his status as a C+ class supernatural being and boasted of his achievements within the Resistance.

Li Yueming, showing his usual calm demeanor, decided to choose a more reserved position within the Resistance. He mentioned that he would prefer a logistics role, as he was inexperienced and didn't excel in combat. Han Longyan agreed and said they could make a formal decision after receiving approval from the leader, Han Longfei.

MO Fei, who had been eavesdropping outside the door, was pleased with Li Yueming's choice and chimed in with support for the logistics position. Han Longyan scolded MO Fei for butting in, but it was clear his words held no real anger.

After considering the situation, Han Longyan agreed to arrange a logistics position for Li Yueming. He explained that they would finalize the specifics after receiving confirmation from Han Longfei.

Li Yueming accepted the offer with a smile and suggested he could work on repairing mechanical equipment since he had some prior knowledge in that area.
