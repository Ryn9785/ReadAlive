He didn't seem to want to say more, and the men from the logistics department didn't press further. He returned to his post and continued working.

On the other hand, Li Yueming discovered a pile of broken parts and began picking them up. Observing this, a member of the logistics department discreetly reminded him, "Ministerâ€¦ Those parts were already scrapped and could only be reforged before they could be used again!"

Li Yueming only smiled and offered no explanation. The logistics department thought he might have some mental issues, but he didn't say anything else and simply blinked.

A month went by, and after Li Yueming had ousted Scarface from the logistics department, nobody came to inquire about him. Nonetheless, the news had still spread.

In just one month, rumors about Li Yueming had circulated throughout the Resistance camp. Some claimed he was the leader's son, considering the critical task he was given right after arriving. Others believed he was a formidable supernatural being due to the incredible strength he'd displayed. With just one arm, he had strangled Scarface, a frequent forger, into submission. Of course, there were those who labeled him a lunatic since he hardly communicated with anyone in the base. Every day, he punched in and out of work on time and had a peculiar obsession with sifting through discarded parts in the logistics department.

However, Li Yueming paid no attention to these rumors. He had been leading a fulfilling life during that month, thanks to his talent as a Wilderness Martial Saint. This ability allowed him to synchronize his attributes with his mecha and weapons. Possessing high-quality weapons and a powerful mech significantly increased his combat prowess.

To escape successfully, he had to destroy his previous mech in N City's underground arena. Li Yueming even cut off some of his own flesh to fake his death. Despite his rapid recovery talent, he had broken into a cold sweat. Han Longfei had been the most frightened witness. He had seen Li Yueming cut off pieces of flesh without any emotion. Fresh blood oozed from the wound and then healed at an astonishing speed, new flesh forming in its place. It almost made Han Longfei regard Li Yueming as a mutant. Hence, upon reaching the Resistance base, Li Yueming's first choice was to join the logistics department, intending to construct a powerful combat mech for himself.

At night, Li Yueming used his superbrain to deduce the necessary structures and materials for the mech. During the day, he searched for resources in the logistics department. He persisted and, after some trials and errors, finally achieved some progress. Within a week, he assembled the first-generation simplified version of the Warrior Sage Mech.

In the third month, Li Yueming started upgrading the Warrior Sage mech with more defensive materials and a variety of weapons. However, Sun Ruo appeared before him.

Li Yueming glanced at her but remained focused on his work. This young girl was a metal-type ability user, and in her eyes, all metal mechs were considered junk. Li Yueming had no way to change her perception except for beating her, which he had no intention of doing.

Seeing his continued concentration on the mech, Sun Ruo pouted, showing her frustration. Nonetheless, she decided to ask, "When are you taking me out for some exercise? I'm almost suffocating from helping out in the child care department here!"

Both Sun Ruo and Li Yueming concealed their true strength. They didn't reveal that they were supernatural beings, and Han Longfei hadn't disclosed their true combat capabilities to the base's higher-ups. Consequently, they were treated like regular people.

Li Yueming was assigned to repair reproductive equipment in the back, while Sun Ruo was tasked with taking care of the children at the base's kindergarten. To be frank, it was quite surprising that Sun Ruo, with her lively personality, could endure two months in child care. Li Yueming couldn't help but find it unbelievable. She looked visibly bored.

Li Yueming raised his head and replied, "At most, one month. I need to repair my mech first and then find an opportunity to practice."

Upon hearing this, Sun Ruo seemed more energized. Observing the mech beneath Li Yueming, she asked, "Can you make one for me too? I'm a Metal Ability user, so I might have an advantage in operating a mech."

Li Yueming glanced at her and nonchalantly said, "I'll make one for you when I have time."

His expression remained unchanged. Sun Ruo then remarked, "No friends, no connections... Why do I feel like you're locking yourself in a cage?"

Upon hearing this, Li Yueming paused in his work.
