Summary

Everyone found themselves transported to a simulated world where every young individual would have the opportunity to experience a new life upon reaching adulthood. Those who could surpass their limitations would permanently become "regressors" with mastery over every aspect of existence. However, those who failed would be relegated to the status of ordinary individuals, reliant on others for protection. Many exercised extreme caution within the simulation to avoid making any mistakes. However, when Li Yueming entered the simulated world of regressors, he discovered that his randomly assigned talent was a 100x Amplification.

Your Blue-rank talent, "Sudden Knowledge," has evolved through the 100x Amplification to become the Gold-rank talent, "Unlimited Deduction."

Unlimited Deduction (Gold): Deduce known information with a 100% success rate.

As he continued to acquire the highest-ranked talents, he suddenly realized that the simulated world, once referred to as hell, wasn't as formidable as it seemed. "I suppose the next one will be a piece of cake too."
